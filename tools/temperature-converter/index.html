Fahrenheit °F

Kelvin K
Absolute zero at 0K, water freezes at 273.15K

Conversion Formulas

Celsius to Fahrenheit
°F = (°C × 9/5) + 32
Fahrenheit to Celsius
°C = (°F - 32) × 5/9
Celsius to Kelvin
K = °C + 273.15
Kelvin to Celsius
°C = K - 273.15
